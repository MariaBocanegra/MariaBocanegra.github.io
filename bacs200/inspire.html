What makes a hero? For me, a hero is not someone that has to save the world or be a "perfect" human being; for me, a hero is someone that makes an impact in other peoples' lives and encourages a positive change. Everyone has their own hero, someone that refelcts their aspirartions and motivates their goals.

The heroes that I most admire have the following characteristics.

  • Strong personality- they are not one to shy away or hide in difficult moments
  • Inspires others - they introduce new forms of creativity
  • Big impact - impacts the life of others, and generations to come
  • Outspoken- they speak up for their beliefs and say what's on their mind
  • Tenacious - they never give up when faced with difficult times

Kanye West has revolutionized the music industry and has created new paths for other artists to express their creativity. From growing up in the violence of Chicago to being recognized as one of the most influential rappers of all time, Kanye West is a true icon that has inspired the lives of many.
